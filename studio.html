KENNEDY STUDIOS

Kennedy is a full service music production and rehearsal studio conventiently located just north of Boston, Massachusetts. Kennedy started as a recording studio, offering services through every step of the production process, from pre-production all the way through the final master. Our operation expanded in 2016 to feature two fully equipped rehearsal rooms with on-site storage and security for bands' equipment. 2020 has brought our new web series: Live At Kennedy, where we attempt to showcase some of our favorite acts from not only New England but around the world.
