KENNEDY STUDIOS

Kennedy is a full service music production and rehearsal studio conventiently located just north of Boston, Massachusetts. Kennedy started as a recording studio, offering services through every step of the production process, from pre-production all the way through the final master. Our operation expanded in 2016 to feature two fully equipped rehearsal rooms with on-site storage and security for bands' equipment. 2020 has brought our new web series: Live At Kennedy, where we attempt to showcase some of our favorite acts from not only New England but around the world. THE STUDIO

KENNEDY STUDIOS

We started our studio in 2012 to be a place where you can come to record your project with no pretense or anxiety. Our promise is to provide musicians with the professional quality recordings they deserve, with engineers that feel like an extension of the band/project. We know what it's like to be on the musician side of the recording equation, and do all that we can to break down that barrier.

MAN MOUNTAIN

MAN MOUNTAIN

Man Mountain is a band of brothers and friends from the southeast corner of Michigan, playing epic and emotive instrumental rock music. Their first set of music was recorded and released in the spring 2013 as an EP titled “To Call Each Thing By Its Right Name”. Since then they’ve played all over Michigan (and thirteen other states), and have written and recorded their first full length album "Infinity Mirror", which was released through Spartan Records on March 16th, 2018.

The band stopped by our studio on their recent North American Tour to play two tracks; "Man of Science, Man of Faith" and "To Be Made As New".

PRAY FOR SOUND

Without ever uttering a word, Pray For Sound channel music’s curative power through instrumental transmissions of lush post-rock, cinematic production, glistening synth pop, and organic tribal percussion. The Boston quartet —Bruce Malley, Steve Aliperta, Joe Aylward, and Nick Stewart—fully realize the scope of that potential to heal on their independent fourth fulllength, Waves.
