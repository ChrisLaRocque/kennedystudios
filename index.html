THE STUDIO

KENNEDY STUDIOS

We started our studio in 2012 to be a place where you can come to record your project with no pretense or anxiety. Our promise is to provide musicians with the professional quality recordings they deserve, with engineers that feel like an extension of the band/project. We know what it's like to be on the musician side of the recording equation, and do all that we can to break down that barrier.

MAN MOUNTAIN

MAN MOUNTAIN

Man Mountain is a band of brothers and friends from the southeast corner of Michigan, playing epic and emotive instrumental rock music. What started in the beginning of 2012 as two people jamming songs by The Appleseed Cast in a basement, turned into four people playing a full set of original songs in someone else’s basement later that summer. That first set of music was recorded and released in the spring 2013 as an EP titled “To Call Each Thing By Its Right Name”. Since then they’ve played all over Michigan (and thirteen other states), and have written and recorded their first full length album "Infinity Mirror", which was released through Spartan Records on March 16th, 2018.

The band stopped by our studio on their recent North American Tour to play two tracks; "Man of Science, Man of Faith" and "To Be Made As New".
